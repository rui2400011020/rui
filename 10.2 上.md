```
n,l=map(int,input().split())
Lantern=set(map(int,input().split()))
Lantern=sorted(Lantern)
n=len(Lantern)
answer=0
for i in range(n-1):
    answer=max(answer,(Lantern[i+1]-Lantern[i])/2)
answer=max(answer,Lantern[0],l-Lantern[-1])
print(answer)
```
